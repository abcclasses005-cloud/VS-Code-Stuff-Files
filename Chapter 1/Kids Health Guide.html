💪Why Your Body Needs Exercise

Your body is like a supercar --- the more you use it, the better it performs. Kids who move for at least 60 minutes a day are more likely to have better physical and mental health.

Dancing, Swimming, and Running are great ways to stay active and healthy!

⭐ Health Tips

🍎 Eat 5 fruits and veggies a day

💧 Drink 8 glasses of water daily

😴 Get 9-11 hours of sleep each night

🏃‍♂️ Move for at least 60 minutes a day
